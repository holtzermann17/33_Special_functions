Gauss’ digamma theorem


Theorem (Gauss).

Ψ⁢(x+n)=1x+1x+1+⋯+1x+n-1+Ψ⁢(x),n=1,2,3,…
Ψ⁢(pq)=-γ-π2⁢cot⁡π⁢pq-ln⁡q+∑n=1q-1cos⁡2⁢π⁢n⁢pq⁢ln⁡(2⁢sin⁡π⁢nq)

where 0<p<q.
