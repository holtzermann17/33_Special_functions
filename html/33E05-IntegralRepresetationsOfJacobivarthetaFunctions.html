integral represetations of Jacobi ϑ functions


The Jacobi theta functions have the following integral representations:

ϑ1(z|τ)=-ei⁢z+i⁢π⁢τ/4∫i-∞i+∞ei⁢π⁢τ⁢u2⁢cos⁡(2⁢u⁢z+π⁢τ⁢u)sin⁡(π⁢u)du
ϑ2(z|τ)=-iei⁢z+i⁢π⁢τ/4∫i-∞i+∞ei⁢π⁢τ⁢u2⁢cos⁡(2⁢u⁢z+π⁢u+π⁢τ⁢u)sin⁡(π⁢u)du
ϑ3(z|τ)=-i∫i-∞i+∞ei⁢π⁢τ⁢u2⁢cos⁡(2⁢u⁢z+π⁢u)sin⁡(π⁢u)du
ϑ4(z|τ)=-i∫i-∞i+∞ei⁢π⁢τ⁢u2⁢cos⁡(2⁢u⁢z)sin⁡(π⁢u)du
