℘-function


Let L be a lattice on ℂ. The Weierstrass ℘ function associated to L is given by

℘⁢(z)=1z2+∑w∈L∖{0}(1(z-w)2-1w2).

The ℘ function is meromorphic and analytic on ℂ∖L, whereas at each w∈L, it has an order 2 pole. It is also an even function, because ℘⁢(z)=℘⁢(-z).

Its derivative

℘′⁢(z)=-2⁢∑w∈L1(z-w)3

is also an odd, meromorphic, and elliptic function, analytic at ℂ∖L and having order 3 poles at each w∈L.

The functions ℘ and ℘′ form together a generator set for the field of elliptic functions associated to the lattice L.
