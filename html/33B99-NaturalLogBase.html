natural log base


The natural log base, or e, has value

2.718281828459045⁢…

e was extensively studied by Euler in the 1720’s, but it was originally discovered by John Napier.

e is defined by

limn→∞⁡(1+1n)n

It is more effectively calculated, however, by using the Taylor series for f⁢(x)=ex at x=1 to get the representation

e=10!+11!+12!+13!+14!+⋯
